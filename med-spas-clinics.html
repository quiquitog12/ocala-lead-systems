The bottleneck

The inquiry comes in. The booking process is where momentum gets lost.

Med spas and clinics often lose money to delayed replies, repetitive front-desk questions, poor follow-up after first contact, and weak reminder flow. The problem usually is not demand. It is the operational path between inquiry and appointment.
