# Cold Email Sequence — Ocala Lead Systems

## Audience
Local businesses with visible lead-handling friction:
- med spas
- clinics
- home services
- roofing
- real estate teams
- high-ticket local service businesses

## Principles
- short
- specific
- outcome-focused
- not too technical
- no hype words unless earned

---

## Email 1 — Simple opener
**Subject options:**
- quick idea for {{company}}
- follow-up question for {{company}}
- possible lead leak at {{company}}
- quick automation idea

**Body:**
Hi {{first_name}},

I took a look at {{company}} and wanted to reach out because businesses like yours often lose leads in the gap between first inquiry and actual follow-up.

I run Ocala Lead Systems. We help local businesses set up AI-powered lead response, follow-up, CRM, and booking systems so inquiries get answered faster and handled more cleanly.

If I sent over 2–3 ideas specific to {{company}}, would that be useful?

— Quiqui
Ocala Lead Systems
352-550-6403
https://ocalaleadsystems.com/ai-automation.html

## Email 2 — Value follow-up
**Subject options:**
- a few ideas for {{company}}
- quick follow-up on automation
- 3 ways {{company}} could tighten lead response

**Body:**
Hi {{first_name}},

Wanted to follow up with a few ideas that usually help businesses like {{company}}:

1. instant response to new inquiries
2. better intake / qualification before staff steps in
3. automatic reminders + cleaner CRM tracking

Most businesses don’t need more software — they need a better system around the leads they already get.

If helpful, I can map a simple version of this for {{company}}.

— Quiqui
Ocala Lead Systems

- should I stop reaching out?

## Optional Hyper-Personalized First Lines
Use one of these before the body if something specific stands out:

- Saw {{company}} offers {{service}} — usually those inquiry flows can be tightened a lot with better first response.
- Noticed you’re clearly active in {{market}}. Businesses in that space often lose momentum between lead capture and callback.
- I looked through {{company}}’s site and had a few ideas around intake, reminders, and lead follow-up.

## Send Cadence
- Day 1: Email 1
- Day 3: Email 2
- Day 6: Email 3
- Day 10: Email 4

## Goal
Do **not** sell the whole system in the first email.
Goal is to get:
- a reply
- permission to send ideas
- or a call booked
